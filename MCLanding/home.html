What is the PMP certification?

The PMP (Project Management Professional) certification is a globally recognized credential offered by the Project Management Institute (PMI). It validates a professional's ability to manage projects and teams, demonstrating expertise in project management methodologies and best practices.

What are the benefits of PMP certification?

The benefits of PMP certification include:

  • Career Advancement: Increased job opportunities and higher earning potential.
  • Industry Recognition: Validates expertise in project management.
  • Networking Opportunities: Access to a global community of professionals.

Flow of the Session

PMP Master Class Agenda

  1. Session Begins & Introductions

    • Brief overview of the session
    • Introductions of the instructor and participants
    • Setting expectations and objectives

  2. All About the PMP Exam

    • Detailed information on the PMP exam structure
    • Eligibility criteria and application process
    • Tips and strategies for exam preparation

  3. Project Management Methodologies

    • Introduction to various project management methodologies
    • How to choose the right methodology?

  4. Agile Approach

    • Overview of Agile principles and practices
    • Key roles and responsibilities in Agile
    • Why Agile project management?

  5. Traditional Approach

    • Explanation of traditional project management methodologies (e.g., Waterfall)
    • Key phases of traditional project management
    • When to use the traditional approach

  6. Hybrid Approach

    • Combining Agile and traditional methodologies
    • Benefits of a hybrid approach
    • Real-world examples of hybrid project management

  7. Interactive Live Simulated Exam Solutions

    • Simulated PMP exam questions and solutions
    • Interactive discussions and problem-solving
    • MOST IMPORTANT: Techniques for tackling difficult exam questions

  8. Q & A Session

    • Open floor for participants to ask questions
    • Clarifications on any topics covered during the session
    • Final tips and best practices for PMP exam success
